Case Study

Yoga & Wellness Mobile App

Context

Concept mobile product designed to organise large volumes of wellness content for time-constrained professionals.

Problem

Users felt overwhelmed by content-heavy navigation and abandoned sessions before completion.

Constraints

  • Mobile-first experience
  • Content-heavy architecture
  • Limited development scope

My Role

UX Research, IA structuring, Wireframing, UI Design.

Process

  • 6 moderated user interviews
  • Card sorting exercise
  • Low-fidelity usability testing
  • Iterative prototyping in Figma

Key Decisions

  • Progressive disclosure navigation
  • Category-based filtering system
  • Reduced cognitive load in task flow

Outcome & Impact

Usability testing showed a 30% faster task completion rate compared to the initial navigation prototype.

Reflection

Future iterations would incorporate habit tracking mechanics to improve long-term engagement.
